HTML Audio and Video

<audio>

The <audio> tag is used to embed audio content in a document.

Example

<audio controls>
    <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

<source>

The <source> tag specifies multiple media resources for elements like <video> and <audio>.

Example

<audio controls>
    <source src="audio.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
    <source src="audio.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
    Your browser does not support the audio element.
</audio>

<track>

The <track> tag provides text tracks for <video> and <audio> elements, such as subtitles or captions.

Example

<video controls>
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <track kind="subtitles" src="subtitles_en.vtt" srclang="en" label="English">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

<video>

The <video> tag is used to embed video content in a document.

Example

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <source src="video.ogg" type="video/ogg">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>
